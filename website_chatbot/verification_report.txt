
QUERY: What are the main clusters for Negamam cotton sarees?
ANSWER: The main clusters visited for Negamam cotton sarees are:

• Vadambacherry
• Negamam
• Peria
• Negamam
• Samathur
• Arani
• Varanasi

QUERY: What is the total carbon footprint of a Baluchari silk saree?
ANSWER: The total carbon footprint of a Baluchari silk saree is approximately 3.99 kg CO₂-equivalent per functional unit.

QUERY: How much does wastewater contribute to Baluchari emissions?
ANSWER: Wastewater generation contributes approximately 19% of total emissions in Baluchari saree production.

QUERY: Compare the weaving techniques of Phulkari and Muslin.
ANSWER: Phulkari uses a handwoven khaddar fabric weaving technique, whereas Muslin uses a Charka-based hand spinning process and handloom weaving technique.
